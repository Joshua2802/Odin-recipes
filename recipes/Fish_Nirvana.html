Recipe:Fish Nirvana

Description:

Chef Suresh Pillai’s legendary fish nirvana recipe. Fish fried in spices and then poached in a banana leaf wrapped ginger coconut milk sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Kingfish steak-1(250 gm)

  • Turmeric powder-1/2 tsp

  • Kasmiri chilli powder-3/4 tsp

  • Pepper powder-1/2 tsp

  • Lemon juice-3/4 tsp

  • Salt-to taste

  • Coconut oil-2 tsp

  • Curry leaves-a few

  • Coconut milk,medium thick-1/3 cup

  • Ginger-1/2 tsp,thinly sliced

  • Green chilies-1 or 2 finely choped

  • Fresh crushed pepper-1/2 tsp

  • Raw mango-2 tsp,peeled and thinly sliced

  • Banana/plantain leaf-1

Steps:

  1. Marinate Kingfish with turmeric powder,kashmiri chilli powder,pepper powder,lemon juice and salt.

  2. Set aside for 30mins.

  3. Heat 2 tsp coconut oil in a pan over medium heat. Place the marinated kingfish and fry for 1 – 2 minutes on each side until half-cooked.

  4. Clean a banana/plantain leaf. Place it over flame for a few seconds to make them soft and pliable

  5. Place the banana leaf inside a clay pot or pan. Heat the clay pot over medium heat. Pour 1 tsp coconut oil on the banana leaf and add in a few torn curry leaves. Place the fried fish on top. Pour 1/3 cup coconut milk on top. Sprinkle sliced ginger, split green chilies, few curry leaves, crushed pepper, raw mango if using and little salt.

  6. Cook for 6 – 8 minutes over low heat.

  7. Once done, add 1 – 2 tsp coconut oil and a few curry leaves.

  8. Serve with appam/ porotta/ plain rice and a stir fry.

  9. Enjoy and lick your fingers in mallu style
